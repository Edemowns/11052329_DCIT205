We believe everyone can be computer a scientists!


With a degree in computer science, you can become the next big thing in our technologically advanced society. Computer scientists have changed the world in which we live. You'll be equipped to develop the next big technological breakthrough with four areas of emphasis, a practical curriculum, a kind and approachable faculty, and lots of work and extracurricular possibilities. With its interactive robotics and visualisation lab, makerspace for creation and discovery, and hands-on networking and security lab, our new technology building will enable possibilities to investigate advanced computer themes.

Our inclusive curriculum starts with the fundamentals of data structures and programming and ends with advanced computer science topics and a capstone project. The programme is also designed to meet you where you are in your computing journey, supporting both students who have never used a computer before and those who have taken computer science courses before enrolling at University of Ghana. Through conferences, hackathons, research opportunities, internships, and an extensive labour programme, computer science majors graduate with practical experience that equips them for success in the modern workforce.

Training Sessions

Our department welcomes senior high school graduates, college students, recent graduates, professionals, and members of the general public who wish to pursue a career in any of the technological sciences for an eight-week intensive certificate programme.
