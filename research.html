CS RESEARCH

A defining characteristic of the University of Ghana is a common commitment for research and education, which is best demonstrated by the undergraduate and graduate computer science programmes. Together with cutting-edge scholarship, research engineers, and students conduct important studies for the government and business.

Our undergraduate and graduate curricula are continuously improved to meet real-world demands and today's rapid technological changes with the aim of equipping students with the skills required for addressing the challenges of the 21st century. An interdisciplinary approach to teaching and research is prevalent throughout the department to enhance the exchange of ideas. The Computer Science Department offers an accredited undergraduate degree in computer science. At the graduate level, we offer M.S. and Ph.D. degree programmes in computer science.


Our faculty members are outstanding educators, committed mentors, and renowned experts in their domains. Our dedication to inclusive excellence, research, and education is one of our guiding principles. I extend an invitation to you, on behalf of the faculty of computer science, to investigate and discover more about the outstanding chances found in our research and teaching initiatives. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions or comments.
