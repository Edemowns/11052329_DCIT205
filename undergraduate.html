Computer Science Undergraduate Program Information

Undergraduate students pursuing the Computer Science programme will receive a strong foundation in the field through a combination of theoretical study, practical computing experience, and scientific and engineering principles. The focus is on computational tasks that are interesting from an intellectual and practical standpoint, as well as on theoretical investigations of effective algorithms and computational boundaries. For independent research, group projects, and class assignments, computers are provided. It is expected of students to look for novel insights in whatever they study. It is highly recommended to collaborate in groups, take part in summer internships, assist student professional organisations, and create multidisciplinary projects.
